Ingredients

  • 1/2 to 3/4 lb. Ground Round
  • 1 bottle V8 juice (46oz.) Low Sodium Best
  • 1 medium onion diced
  • 1 can diced petite tomatoes with basil, oregano, and/or garlic
  • 3/4 cup thinly diced celery heart
  • pepper
  • 1 cup elbow or cut macaroni
  • dash of parmesan
  • 1/2 cup corn (optional)

Cooking Instructions

  • Cook beef and drain
  • Cook macaroni and drain
  • In large pan, combine ingredients and simmer on low heat approx. 15-20 minutes
  • Add pepper to taste
