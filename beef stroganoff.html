Beef Stroganoff

  • Beef Stroganoff

Ingredients

  • 2lb. beef stew meat
  • 12 oz. white mushrooms, cleaned and sliced
  • 2 tablespoons minced fresh garlic
  • 1 1/2 cups beef broth
  • 2 tablespoons red wine (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tablespoon dijon mustard
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 2 tablespoons dried parsley (or 1/2 cup fresh parsley)
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 6 tablespoons flour
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 4 oz. cream cheese at room temperature
  • 8 oz. sour cream
  • Salt and pepper
  • Egg noodles

Cooking Instructions

  • Place beef, mushrooms, and garlic in crock pot
  • In medium bowl, mix together beef broth, red wine, worcestershire sauce, mustard, garlic powder, onion powder, dried parsley, thyme and flour.
  • Pour mixture from bowl into crock pot, add bay leaf, and mix all ingredients
  • Place lid on crock pot and cook for 8 to 10 hours on low (4 to 5 hours on high)
  • ~20 minutes before serving, add cream cheese to crockpot and replace lid
  • ~10 minutes before serving, stir softened cream cheese into sauce and stir sour cream into sauce
  • Continue to cook on low for ~10 more minutes or until heated through
  • Boil egg noodles according to package instructions
  • Remove bay leaf from stroganoff, salt and pepper as desired
  • Serve over hot egg noodles and garnish with additional minced parsley, if desired

Tips and Tricks

  • For beef broth, use 1 1/2 cups water plus 2 teaspoons Organic Better Than Bouillon Beef Base
  • If using 1/2 cup fresh minced parsley, add at the same time as the sour cream is added as opposed to the beginning
  • The cream cheese makes the stroganoff sauce extra creamy, but it may be left out to make the dish lighter
