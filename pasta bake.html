  • Southwest Salad

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. ground beef
  • 1 box Rotini noodles
  • l large jar sauce (45oz.?)
  • 1 lb. mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesean/Romero cheese

Cooking Instructions

  • Boil noodles and drain
  • Brown beef and drain
  • Shred mozzarella while noodles boil
  • Combine noodles, cheese, sauce and beef in large saucepan, mix
  • Spread in baking pan
  • Sprinkle parmesan on top
  • Bake on 350 for approx 20-30 minutes or until cheese begins to brown
