Ingredients

  • 1 box lasagna noodles
  • 4 tablespoon extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 1 lb italian sausage
  • 1/2 cup white wine
  • 2 jars marinara sauce
  • 1 15oz. container fresh ricotta cheese
  • 1/2 cup parmesean cheese, grated
  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese, diced
  • Salt and Pepper to taste

Cooking Instructions

  • Preheat oven to 375
  • Cook pasta according to package instructions
  • In large skillet, sautee onions in olive oil until translucent. Add sausage and cook through
  • Deglaze with wine and reduce
  • Add 1 1/3 jars of marinara sauce to the sausage mixture, bring to a boil
  • Spray 13x9 baking dish with cooking spray
  • Pour half of the remaining marinara sauce on the bottom
  • Layer 5 sheets of lasagna, top with 1/3 of sausage mixture, 1/3 ricotta, and 1/3 parmesean cheese. Repeat 2 more times
  • On top layer, pour remaining marinara sauce and cover with foil
  • Bake in preheated oven for 20 minutes
  • Top with mozzarella and remaining parmesean cheese
  • Re-cover with foil and continue baking for ~10 minutes
  • Before slicing, let rest 5 minutes
